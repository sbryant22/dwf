Heads Vs. Tails

headsvstails.com

Markup Review

Heads Vs. Tails is a free app game of flipping coins. It can be found in the Apple App Store. It is a realistic coin tossing game with a full 3D engine. It has custom coins through 12 themes and it has two unnoticable ways to force the draw. You can also check the rates of your draws and your progress.

This site has not made any kind of attempt at tabbing/indenting their markup. They have used the html structure elements like <nav>, <header>, and <ul> correctly. They have 19 errors in their code. Some of their issues are that they are using unregistered keywords and some attributes are missing.

A way that can fix some of these problems would be to use registered keywords or to register them theirselves. To fix the other problems, the developer needs to fix the ones that missing attributes. They also shouldn't use the <nav> twice.

Their outline came out good. All their headings and subheadings are labeled in order of how it appears on the site.

Justin Timberlake

justintimberlake.com

Markup Review

Justin Timberlake's website is where fans of his can go to catch up on the latest news and events.

This site's markup makes good use of indenting/tabbing but not so much with HTML5 structural elements like the <nav>, <header>, <body>, and <section> tags. Some of the closing <p> tags are used incorrectly. They do not use the proper entities for ampersands in their url links. This site has 197 errors.

To fix some of these errors, I would elimnate the too many hypens used in the comments and I would close them. A lot of the errors are because the developered used a lot of hypens in the comments possibly thinking that it would close them. Some errors are also that some of the old html for presentations need to be replaced with CSS. I would also fix the closing tags that don't stem from opened tags.

This site has some issues in their outline. There are a lot of untitled sections where headers and sub headers should be.

Remaniax

remaniax.com

Markup Review

This website is a music site. Real house music by Remaniax. This site has news and events about the band. It also has downloads of their music and booking information.

The developer for this site made good use of indenting/tabbing and overall good use of html5 structure elements. This site only has 9 errors. The errors are mostly that some image attributes are missing and a few keywords are not registered. To fix these errors I would register the keywords or use words that are already and to fix the missing attributes.

The outline for this came out correctly. All the headings and subheadings are correct and in the order that is seen on their website.

Markup Review

To fix these issues, I would but the correct HTML5 entities for the ampersand and I would correct the bad attributes for the meta and links tags.
