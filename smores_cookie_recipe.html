Gooey S'mores Cookies

What you'll need:

First, you will need:

  • 1/2 cup (1 stick) butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup of sugar
  • 1/4 cup of brown sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 1/3 cups of flour
  • 3/4 cup of graham cracker crumbs
  1. (7 full graham cracker sheets)
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 cup of Mini Hershey's Kisses
  1. (or chocolate chips/chunks)
  • 1 cup of Jet Puffed Mallow Bits

Directions:

Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Line a cookie sheet with parchment paper and spray with cooking spray. Using a mixer, cream the butter and sugars until light and fluffy, about 3 minutes. Add the eggs and vanilla and beat for an additional 2 minutes.

Meanwhile, use a food processor or blender to crush the 7 graham cracker sheets into 3/4 cup of crumbs. In a separate bowl, whisk together the graham cracker crumbs, flour, baking powder, and salt. Slowly add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, and mix on low speed until dough just comes together. Add the chocolate and marshmallows and stir to combine.

Place 1.5 tablespoon sized scoops of dough onto the prepared cookie sheet. Bake at 350 for 12 minutes.

Makes 2 dozen cookies
